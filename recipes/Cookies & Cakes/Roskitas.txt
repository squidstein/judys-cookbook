# Roskitas

Prep. Time: 1hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                                                 Yield: 48

INGREDIENTS

- One egg recipe
- 1 egg
- 3 level tbsp. sugar
- 3 level tbsp. milk
- 3 level tbsp. oil
- 1 level tsp. baking powder
- for every 3 eggs, add 1 tsp. vanilla
- flour

INSTRUCTIONS

Beat together liquid ingredients with eggs.

In a separate bowl, mix together flour and baking powder.

Only add enough flour to make the dough as soft as possible.

Let dough rest for ½ hour.

Coat hands with flour so dough will not stick to hands.

Roll out and shape into doughnuts.

Fry.
