# Lemon Poppy Seed Supreme

Prep. Time: 30 mins.
From Ann K’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- 1 pkg lemon cake mix
- 1 pkg lemon instant pudding
- 4 eggs
- ½ cup oil
- 1 c. water
- 1 tsp. lemon peel
- poppy seeds (¼ c?)
- Topping
- ¼ c. chopped nuts
- 1 tsp. cinnamon
- ¼ c. icing sugar

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 350°

Modify directions on box of cake mix to add the above ingredients.  Bake for one hour.

Add topping, if desired.
