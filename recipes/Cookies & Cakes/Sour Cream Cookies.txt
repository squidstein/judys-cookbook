# Sour Cream Cookies

EMBED Word.Picture.8
Preparation Time: ½hr
Meshu’s Kitchen				   Yield: 5 dozen

INGREDIENTS

- ½ c. shortening		1 tsp. soda
- 2 c. sugar			1 c. sour cream
- 2 eggs				1 tsp vanilla
- 4 c. flour			½ tsp. salt
- ¼ c. sugar- cinnamon mixture

INSTRUCTIONS

Cream together shortening and sugar.

Add vanilla.

Add eggs and beat until fluffy.

Sift together flour, salt and soda.

Add sifted ingredients alternately with sour cram.

Mix thoroughly.  Drop by teaspoon onto greased sheet.  Sprinkle with sugar-cinnamon mixture.

Bake at 375° for about 12 minutes.
