# Passover Macaroons

From Jana’s Kitchen                                     Prep. Time: 15 mins.

INGREDIENTS

- ½ lb. (1½ cups) blanched almonds ground very fine
- ⅔ c. sugar
- ½ c. egg whites (approx. 3-4)
- ½ tsp. Almond extract
- Slivered blanched almonds

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven 350°

In a medium sized bowl, combine the ground almonds with the sugar.  Set aside.

Beat eggs whites until stiff but not dry.  Add the almond extract near the end of the beating.  Fold the stiffly beaten egg whites into the almond-sugar mixture.

Place the nut mixture in a pastry bag, press out 1½" rosettes 1" apart onto a foil lined cookie sheet.  (Or drop by teaspoonful) Top each with a few almond slivers.

Bake in preheated 350 oven for 20 minutes, until edges are golden.  Let stand for about 5 minutes.  Peel from foil and transfer to racks for cooling.

Yields about 30.
