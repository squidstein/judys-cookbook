# Fijuelas

Prep. Time: 2hrs
From Grandma’s Kitchen                                    Yield: approx. 50

INGREDIENTS

- 6 eggs
- ⅔ c. oil
- 1 ⅓ c. water
- ½ tsp. baking powder
- 2 Tbsp sugar
- pinch of salt
- flour

INSTRUCTIONS

Add enough flour until it becomes a ball.  DO NOT overwork dough.

Cover and let rest for ½ hour.

Roll out slightly.

Cut into strips.  Cut strips about 2” long.

Pass through pasta maker at various widths until you get long thin strips. Place on the table to dry out slightly.  Pierce with fork at one end and fry slightly as you continue to roll.

This recipe can be prepared in any batch size.  The ingredients are relational to the number of eggs in the recipe.

For each egg, add:

1 - ½ eggshell of oil

2 - ½ eggshell of water

Almeba (Syrup)

2 cups sugar

1 cup honey

2 cups water

1 tsp. vanilla

Combine sugar, honey and water.  Bring to boil then lower heat or mixture can boil over.  Continue to cook until thick enough.  Test for hardness by dropping a drop on counter and it holds it’s shape.  Add vanilla when honey is removed from heat.
