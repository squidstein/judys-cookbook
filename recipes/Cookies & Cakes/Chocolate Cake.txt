# Chocolate Cake

Preparation Time: ½hr
From Mrs. Martin’s Kitchen                    Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- ¼ lb. shortening
- 1¼ c. white sugar
- 2 eggs
- 3 heaping Tbsp. cocoa
- 1 c. sour milk
- 1¾ c. flour
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 tsp. vanilla

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 375°.

Cream shortening and sugar together and beat in eggs.  Add cocoa.

Dissolve soda in sour milk and add to mixture.

Work in flour slowly.

Add vanilla.

Beat until smooth.

Place in well-greased pan.

Bake at 375° for 30 minutes.  Start baking at 300°.

For sour milk – add 1 Tbsp. white vinegar to sweet milk and let stand for 15 minutes before making cake.
