# Chocolate Chip Toll House Cookies

Preparation Time: ½hr
From Meshu’s Kitchen                         Number of servings: 24-36

INGREDIENTS

- ½ c. butter				½ tsp. baking soda
- ½ c. brown sugar			1½ c. chocolate chips
- ½ c. white sugar			1 Tbsp. hot water
- 1 egg					1 tsp vanilla
- 1½ c. flour				½ c. chopped nuts
- ½ tsp. salt

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 375°.

Cream butter and sugars together.  Add egg, water and vanilla and beat until fluffy.  Sift flour.  Measure and add salt and baking soda.  Sift again.  Add dry ingredients.  Beat again until smooth.  Add chocolate ships and nuts.  Blend into mixture.

Drop by teaspoon onto greased cookie sheets, 2” to 3” apart.

Bake at 375° for 10 minutes or until light brown.

You can’t keep the kids away from these.
