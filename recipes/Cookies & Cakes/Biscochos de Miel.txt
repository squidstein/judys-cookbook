# Biscochos de Miel

Prep. Time: ½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                                    Yield: 48 cookies

INGREDIENTS

- 6 eggs
- 1 c. oil *
- 1 c. sugar *
- 4 Tbsp. honey
- 3 tsp. Baking Powder (level)
- 1½ tsp. cinnamon (level)
- ¾ tsp. ground cloves
- 4 - 4½ cups flour

INSTRUCTIONS

* cup refers to Duralex cup – approximately 9 ounces (not imperial or metric, but Moroccan)

Preheat oven to 325°.

When breaking eggs, reserve one egg yolk for coating the top of the cookies.

Beat eggs.

Add sugar, oil then honey.

In a separate bowl mix 1 cup of flour with cinnamon, ground cloves and baking powder.

Add flour mixture to egg mixture.  Continue to add flour until mixture forms soft dough.

Lubricate hands with oil.  Form balls with dough.

Using your finger, coat tops of cookies with reserved egg yolk.

Bake at 325° until egg coating on cookies turns golden.
