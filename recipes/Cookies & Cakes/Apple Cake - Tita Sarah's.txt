# Apple Cake - Tita Sarah's

Prep. Time: ½ hr
From Tita Sarah’s Kitchen                     Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- ¼ lb. margarine (½ cup)
- 1 c sugar
- 1½ tsp vanilla
- 3 eggs
- 1½ c. flour
- 1 tsp. baking powder
- 6 apples
- 1 small jar peach jam

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 350°

Beat margarine and sugar until fluffy

Add eggs, then vanilla

Mix flour and baking powder in separate bowl

Combine dry and wet ingredients.

Grease a spring form pan.  Pour in batter.  Insert sliced apples upright throughout the entire cake

Bake at  350° for one hour.

Let cool then spread peach jam over surface.
