# Apple Crisp

Preparation Time: 30 mins.
From Camila’s Kitchen                         Number of servings: 6 - 8

INGREDIENTS

- 6 – 8 apples
- ½ cup margarine
- ¾ c. brown sugar
- ¾ c. rolled oats
- ¾ c. flour
- 1 tsp. cinnamon
- ½ chopped walnuts (optional)
- 1 tsp. lemon juice

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 375°

Peel and slice apples.

Fill oblong Pyrex dish with apples.  Dot with margarine. Sprinkle evenly with lemon juice.  Add a bit more lemon juice if you prefer a more tart taste.

Add walnuts, if using.

Sprinkle with additional cinnamon.

Top with crumble mix (brown sugar, flour, oats and margarine).

Do not press down.

Bake at  350° for 30 – 40 minutes.  Apple mixture should be bubbling.
