# Forgotten Cookies

From Rhonda Stein’s Kitchen             Preparation Time: 15 mins.

INGREDIENTS

- 2 egg whites
- ¾ c. sugar
- 1 tsp. Vanilla
- ¼ tsp. Salt
- 1 c. chopped pecans
- 1 c. chocolate chips

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven 350°

Line 2 cookie sheets with parchment paper.

With electric mixer, beat egg whites until peaks form.

Add sugar slowly (about 1 tablespoon at a time) Add vanilla and salt and beat until everything is very stiff and very shiny.

Fold pecans and chocolate chips.  Drop by teaspoons onto the prepared cookie sheets and place in preheated oven.  Turn off the oven and “forget” the cookies until the next morning.

Makes about 3 dozen.

Put sign on oven – “DO NOT TURN ON.  DO NOT OPEN”

These are GREAT for Passover
