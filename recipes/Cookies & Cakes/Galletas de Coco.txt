# Galletas de Coco

Preparation Time: ½hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                  Number of servings: not enough

INGREDIENTS

- 6 eggs
- 1 c. sugar
- ½ c. oil
- 3 tsp. (level) baking powder
- ½ lb. fine unsweetened coconut
- flour

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven 350°

Beat eggs, add sugar, and then add oil.

In a separate bowl, mix baking powder with flour.

Combine flour mixture with egg mixture and add flour as needed.  Dough should be firm enough to roll out.

Cut with cookie cutter or cut in diamond shaped strips.

Bake until lightly coloured.  Do not over bake.

Kids can help with these.  They’re easy to handle – the cookies, not the kids.
