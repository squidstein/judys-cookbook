# Sour Cream Coffee Cake

From Julie Hazan’s Kitchen                                 Prep. Time: ½hr

INGREDIENTS

- 1 cup butter
- 1 cup sugar
- 3 eggs
- 3 tsp. baking powder
- 2½ cups flour
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 tsp. vanilla
- Streusel topping
- ¾ cup brown sugar
- ½ cup chopped nuts (pecans are best)
- 1 tbsp. cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 350°.

Cream together butter and sugar.  Add eggs.  Alternating add dry ingredients and sour cream.  Add vanilla.

Pour ½ batter into GREASED/ FLOURED bundt cake or tube spring form.

Sprinkle with ½ streusel topping.

Pour remaining ½ of batter into pan, on top of streusel.

Sprinkle with remaining streusel.

Bake at 350° until done

(less than 1 hour)
