# Lemon Bundt Cake

Preparation Time: ½hr
In the Sweet Kitchen                            Number of servings: 10-12

INGREDIENTS

- 1 c. butter, at room temperature
- 2 cups granulated sugar
- 4 eggs, at room temperature
- 3 cups all-purpose flour
- ½ tsp. baking soda
- ½ tsp. salt
- 1 c. buttermilk or soured milk
- Grated zest of 2 lemons
- Juice of 1 lemon (about 2½ tbsp.)
- Lemon Glaze – recipe follows

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 325°. Grease 10-inch bundt pan.

Using wooden spoon, or the paddle attachment of an electric mixer, cream butter and sugar together in a large bowl until fluffy and pale.  Add eggs, one at a time, beating well after each addition, and scraping down sides of the bowl.

Sift flour, baking soda and salt into small bowl.  Add flour mixture to butter mixture in three additions, alternating with buttermilk. Beginning and ending with flour mixture.  Don’t over mix, folding gently until well blended.  Fold in lemon zest and juice.

Scrape batter into prepared pan, smoothing tip with rubber spatula.  Bake 1 to 1¼ hours, or until tester inserted into center comes out clean, and cake has begun to pull away from sides of pan.

Cool on wire rack 10 minutes.

To Glaze, invert cake onto wire rack set over baking sheet.  Spoon glaze over hot cake.  Cool completely before cutting.  Keeps in an airtight container at room temperature for up to 3 days.

Makes 10 to 12 servings.

Lemon Glaze

¼ c. butter, softened

1 – 2 cups icing sugar, sifted

Finely grated zest of 1 large or 2 small lemons

Juice of 1 lemon (about 2½ tbsp.)

In medium bowl, cream butter until light and fluffy.  Alternately and gradually, beat in sugar and lemon juice until creamy and pourable.  Use immediately.

Makes about 2 cups.

For sour milk – add 1 Tbsp. white vinegar to sweet milk and let stand for 15 minutes before making cake.
