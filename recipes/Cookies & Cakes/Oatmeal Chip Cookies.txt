# Oatmeal Chip Cookies

Prep. Time: ½hr
From Meshu’s Kitchen                                  Yield: 3½ - 4 dozen

INGREDIENTS

- ½ c. butter				½ tsp. baking soda
- ½ c. brown sugar			½ tsp. salt
- ½ c. white sugar			1 c. rolled oats
- ½ tsp. vanilla				1 c. chocolate chips
- 1 egg					½ c. chopped nuts
- 1 c. flour

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 375°.

Cream butter and sugars together, add vanilla. Beat in egg then water.  Sift together flour, baking soda and salt.  Add to creamed mixture, blending well.  Stir in oats, chocolate and nuts.

Drop by teaspoon onto greased cookie sheets.

Bake at 375° for 10 – 12 minutes or until light brown.
