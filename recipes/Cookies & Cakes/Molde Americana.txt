# Molde Americana

Prep. Time: ½hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                     Number of servings: 10-12

INGREDIENTS

- 9 eggs
- 500 grams ground hazelnut
- 2 cups sugar*
- 3 Tbsp matzo meal (heaping)

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 325°.

Separate eggs.

In another bowl, mix together the ground hazelnuts and matzo meal.  Set aside.

Beat egg whites until stiff.  Gradually add sugar.  Mix well then add egg yolks.

Fold dry ingredients into egg mixture.

Line cake pan with wax or parchment paper.  Do not grease.

Bake at 325° for one hour.

When cooled, either sprinkle with icing sugar or cover with chocolate frosting.

* Tita Sarah uses 3 cups of sugar.  Mom’s recipe is not as sweet.
