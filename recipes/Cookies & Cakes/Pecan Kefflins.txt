# Pecan Kefflins

From Kay Selmes’s Kitchen                                             Yield: 36

INGREDIENTS

- 1 c. butter
- ½ c. icing
- 1 tbsp. cold water
- 1 tbsp. vanilla extract
- 2 c. sifted flour
- 1 c. chopped pecans
- semi-sweet chocolate squares

INSTRUCTIONS

Blend together all ingredients.  Dough should be soft.  Place in refrigerator until firm.  Shape with hands into crescents or horseshoes.

Bake at 350° for 15 minutes.

Melt semi-sweet chocolate squares in a double boiler or Pyrex bowl placed in hot not boiling water.

Dip half the cookie into chocolate.  Place on waxed paper until dry.
