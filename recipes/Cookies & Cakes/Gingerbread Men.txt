# Gingerbread Men

From Meshu’s Kitchen                         Yield: 24 gingerbread men

INGREDIENTS

- ¾ c. crisco			1 tsp. baking powder
- ½ c. sugar			1 tsp. baking soda
- ½ c. molasses			1 tsp. cinnamon
- 1egg				1 tsp. ground cloves
- 3 c. flour			1 tsp. ginger
- ½ tsp. salt			½ tsp nutmeg

INSTRUCTIONS

Cream shortening, sugar, molasses and egg.  Mix well.

Sift together dry ingredients.

Blend into creamed mixture.

Chill overnight or at least a few hours.

Roll ¼” thick on lightly floured board and cut with cookie cutter.

Bake at 350° for 8 to 10minutes.

Cool on rack.

Decorative Icing

4 c. icing sugar

1 tsp. cream of tarter

3 egg whites

In a large bowl, combine confectioners sugar, egg whites and cream of tarter.  Beat at high speed in mixer until frosting is very thick and holds it’s shape (about 5 minutes)

Cover with damp cloth to prevent it from drying out.

Makes about 3 cups.
