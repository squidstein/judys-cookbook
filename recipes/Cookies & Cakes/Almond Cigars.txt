# Almond Cigars

Preparation Time: 1½ hrs
Number of servings: 12

INGREDIENTS

- 1 lb. blanched almonds
- 1 c. sugar
- 1 c. water
- lemon juice (squirt)
- rind from 1 lemon
- 2 egg yolks
- 2 pkgs. Phyllo pastry

INSTRUCTIONS

Add grated rind of one lemon to 1 lb. ground almond.

Bring water and sugar to boil (not too long or sugar will harden).  Add a squirt of lemon juice.

Add almond mixture to sugar mixture.  Return to low heat and stir.  Mixture should be liquid in consistency.  Stir until it thickens a little (not too long) Remove from heat.  Continue to stir (consistency will thicken).

Add 2 egg yolks.

Cut phyllo into strips as long as the desired length of the cigar and should be wide enough to wrap around cigar 4 to 5 times.  Place mixture on phyllo at the edge and roll.  Moisten end of phyllo cigar with water with the end of your finger to seal.

These can be made to this point and frozen for up to one month.

Deep fry in very hot oil for only 2 minutes (taking care that they not burn or become too dark).  If oil is not hot enough or too shallow, cigars will become oily.

Do not dip in almeba while they are still hot or they will become soggy.

Almeba (Syrup)

½ c. water

1 c. sugar

squirt of lemon

3 tbsp. honey

Bring water and sugar to a boil.  This mixture should thicken a bit then add a dash of lemon.  Add 3 tbsp honey.  When it starts to foam and boil remove from heat.
