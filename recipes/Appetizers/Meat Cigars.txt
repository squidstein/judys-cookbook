# Meat Cigars

Prep. Time: 1½ hrs
Grandma’s Kitchen			                  Servings: 12

INGREDIENTS

- 1 lb. lean ground beef
- 1 pkg phyllo pastry
- ½ tsp garlic
- ½ tsp cumin
- ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
- ¼ tsp salt
- ¼ c. water

INSTRUCTIONS

Add water to meat in pot.

Add spices and cook on low heat, stirring occasionally until water has evaporated.

Allow to cool.

Mix in food processor – just enough to change texture of meat.

Cut phyllo pastry and roll out individual cigars with meat inside.

Seal with water on edges of phyllo.

Can be frozen at this point.

Fry in a little oil.  Oil should not be too hot or outside will burn and inside will not cook properly.
