# Sauteed Cherry Tomatoes

Recipe from Perfect Recipes                        	         Serves 6

INGREDIENTS

- 2 Tbsp Olive oil
- 2 pints cherry or grape tomatoes
- Salt and freshly ground black pepper
- 2 Garlic cloves, minced
- 1 Tbsp minced fresh basil
- 1 Tbsp extra-virgin olive oil

INSTRUCTIONS

Heat oil in a large (12-inch) skillet over medium high heat.  Until it just starts to smoke.  Add tomatoes and season with salt and pepper.  Sauté, shaking pan frequently, until tomatoes soften and skins just start to wrinkle, about 2 minutes.  Stir in garlic and continue to shake pan until garlic is fragrant.

Stir in basil and extra-virgin olive oil and serve.
