# Boregas

Prep. Time: 1½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen	                                   Yield: 48+

INGREDIENTS

- 2 onions, chopped
- 1 pkg spinach, steamed, chopped
- 6 potatoes, boiled
- 2 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- 2 pkgs Concord Puff Pastry

INSTRUCTIONS

Sauté onions.

Boil and mash potatoes.

Steam spinach.

Beat eggs and add the above ingredients plus salt and pepper.  If mixture is too hard add another egg or just egg white and save yolk to brush on top of the boregas before baking.

Thaw pastry.  Cut in half.  Roll out quite thinly and cut in strips then in squares.   Fill with filling, wet edges and fold over.

Bake at 300° until golden.

Freeze very well.
