# Mango Salsa

1 large red bell pepper
2 scallions
honey
fresh lemon juice
DIRECTIONS

INSTRUCTIONS

In medium bowl, gently stir together 1 mango, cut into 1/2-inch cubes, 1 large red bell pepper, cut into 1/2-inch pieces, 2 thinkly sliced scallions, 2 tablespoons honey, and 1 tablespoon fresh lemon juice.
