# Mini Cocktail Pizzas

Prep. Time: 1½ hr
Recipe from Presidential Gourmet                        Servings: 10-12

INGREDIENTS

- pesto (recipe following)
- pizza dough
- red onion
- artichoke hearts
- feta cheese

INSTRUCTIONS

Roll out pizza dough to ⅛” thick.

Cut with 2” round cutter.

Bake on cookie sheet until slightly puffed. Let cool.

Smear with pesto.

Garnish with ⅛th artichoke heart, slice of red onion and crumbled feta cheese.

Bake at 350° for 3 to 5 minutes.

Basil Pesto

This puréed basil base makes a perfect crostini spread, or you can add ground pine nuts and Parmesan for a truly lush pesto.

4 cloves garlic, minced

2 cups fresh basil leaves, rinsed and thoroughly dried

⅓ cup extra virgin olive oil

½ teaspoon salt

½ teaspoon freshly ground black pepper

Place the garlic and basil in the bowl of a food processor.  With the motor running, slowly drizzle in the oil through the feed tube, and process until the basil is puréed.

Transfer the pesto to a bowl and stir in the salt and pepper.  Refrigerate, covered, until ready to use.  This keeps in the refrigerator for 2 to 3 days.  Makes ¾ cup.
