# Meatballs

(so easy even kids can make them)
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

- 1 lb. lean ground beef
- 1 egg
- salt, to taste
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- breadcrumbs or matzo meal
- turmeric
- oil

INSTRUCTIONS

Heat oil.  Add turmeric then water.  When it returns to a boil, add meatballs and return to boil again. Then add vegetables.
