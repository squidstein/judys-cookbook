# Roasted Brisket

Preparation Time: 15 mins
From Grandma’s Kitchen		   Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- brisket
- salt
- pepper
- nutmeg
- garlic powder
- 2 bay leaves
- 2 sliced onions

INSTRUCTIONS

Place brisket in pan without any water. Add spices and place sliced onions over roast.  Cover and roast in oven at

300° - 350°.

After 2 hours, check with fork, if tender, remove.  If not, continue cooking until tender.
