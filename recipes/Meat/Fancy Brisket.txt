# Fancy Brisket

INGREDIENTS

- 5-7 lb brisket, washed and drained
- 1/2 cup oil
- 1/4 cup Coca-Cola
- 1/2 cup dry red wine
- 1/4 cup honey
- 4 Tbsp. ketchup
- 1/2 tsp. mustard powder
- 1 small onion
- 1/2 tsp. paprika

INSTRUCTIONS

Chop all seasonings in food processor and pour over brisket. Best if marinated overnight.

Cook at 350 degrees Fahrenheit, loosely covered, until done, approximately 4 to 5 hours. When cool, pour the whole gravy in a saucepan. Add 1 Tbsp. flour and let cook until half the liquid is reduced. Pour over sliced meat.
