# Stuff Zucchini's

From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

- 1 lb. lean ground beef
- 1 egg
- salt, to taste
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- breadcrumbs or matzo meal
- turmeric
- oil
- Onion

INSTRUCTIONS

Heat oil.  Fry onion. Cut and quarter zucchini.  Use baller to scoop out inside.  Fill zucchini with meat.  Add insides to fired onion.  Add turmeric and water 1 cup.  Then place filled zucchini on top of filling.    Add turmeric then water.
