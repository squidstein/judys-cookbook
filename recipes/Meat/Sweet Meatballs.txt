# Sweet Meatballs

From Sylvia’s Kitchen

INGREDIENTS

- 2 lb. lean ground beef (or veal)
- 2 eggs
- ½ c. ketchup
- salt
- pepper
- garlic powder
- breadcrumbs or matzo meal

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine above ingredients, adding enough matzo meal to give meat consistency to form balls.  Drop into boiling sauce and reduce heat and cook on low for 1 - 1½ hrs.

Sauce

½ c. brown sugar

2 c’s ketchup

juice of one lemon

1 – 10 oz can of sprite, ginger ale or 7-up

Combine sauce ingredients.  Bring to a boil then drop in meatballs.
