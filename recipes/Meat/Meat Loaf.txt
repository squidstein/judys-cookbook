# Meat Loaf

Preparation Time: 1hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen 	                Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- 2½ lbs. ground beef/ veal mixture
- 2 eggs
- ½ c. rolled oats
- 2 medium onions sautéed
- 1 green pepper sautéed
- ½ c. – ¾ c. tomato sauce
- 1 tsp. salt
- ¼ tsp. pepper
- ¼ tsp nutmeg
- ¼ tsp garlic powder, or if using 2 fresh garlic, sauté with onions

INSTRUCTIONS

Sauté onions, drain and set aside.

Sauté green peppers, drain and set aside.

Brush loaf pan with oil from onions.  Reserve remaining oil for sauce.  Mix all remaining ingredients well.  Place in loaf pan.  Cover pan with foil.

Bake at 375° for 1½ hours.

Sauce

1 tbsp oil reserved from onions

½ tbsp. paprika

14 oz can tomato sauce
