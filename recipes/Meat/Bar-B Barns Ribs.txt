# Bar-B Barns Ribs

Preparation Time: ½hr
From: Bar-B Barns, Montreal 	                    Yield: 3 cups sauce

INGREDIENTS

- 1 c. applesauce
- ½ c. ketchup
- 2 c. packed brown sugar
- 6 Tbsp. lemon juice
- ½ tsp. salt
- ½ tsp. pepper
- ½ tsp. powdered garlic or 2 crushed cloves
- ½ tsp. cinnamon

INSTRUCTIONS

Heat to boiling.

Let cool.

Pour over ribs.

Let stand in fridge overnight or for at least a few hours.

BBQ or broil.

Ribs:

Boil for ½ hour, cut into individual portions.  Let cool.
