# Emeril's Ribs

2 racks baby back pork ribs, about 4 pounds, each cut in half
2 tablespoons  HYPERLINK "http://www.wchstv.com/gmarecipes/essence.shtml" Essence or favorite rib rub
1 (12-ounce) bottle dark brown ale (recommended: Abita Turbo Dog)
2 cups ketchup
1/4 cup molasses
1/4 cup brown sugar
2 tablespoons Worcestershire sauce
1 tablespoon grated ginger
2 teaspoons sesame oil
2 teaspoons soy sauce
2 teaspoons chopped garlic
1 teaspoon chili powder
Sliced fresh tomatoes, accompaniment
