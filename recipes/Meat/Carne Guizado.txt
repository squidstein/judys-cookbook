# Carne Guizado

(English Subtitle – cooked meat)
Preparation Time: 15 mins
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- 2 lbs. beef – chuck
- oil
- turmeric
- 2 bay leaves
- pepper
- nutmeg
- garlic powder
- salt, to taste
- water

INSTRUCTIONS

In pressure cooker, heat oil.  Add turmeric then meat.  Add remaining spices, then enough water to cover meat.

Place lid on pressure cooker and cook for 15 minutes after whistle starts.

When lid is removed, check for tenderness with fork.  If not tender enough continue to cook.  If tender but too much water, remove lid and continue to cook until water evaporates.
