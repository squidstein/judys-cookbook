# Pulpetas en Cororado

(English Subtitle – hamburgers in red)
Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen		Number of servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

- 1 lb. lean ground beef
- 1 egg
- pepper
- cumin
- parsley
- breadcrumbs or matzo meal
- salt
- ½ tin tomato sauce
- ½ tbsp paprika
- ¼ tsp cayenne pepper
- (NO garlic powder in this recipe)

INSTRUCTIONS

Heat paprika in oil.  Add ½ tin tomato sauce.  Rinse out tin with water and add this liquid to pot.  Add cayenne pepper.

Bring to a mild boil.  (the other half of the tomato sauce can be put in the fridge or freezer for later use).

Place pulpetas in pot.  Cover and cook for 20 minutes.
