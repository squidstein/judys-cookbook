# Burgers

Preparation Time: 10 mins
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- Beef					Chicken
- 2 lbs. ground (medium) beef		2 lbs. ground chicken
- 1 tsp. salt				1 tsp. salt
- ½ tsp. pepper				½ tsp. pepper
- 1 tsp. parsley				1 tsp. parsley
- ¼ tsp. cumin				¼ tsp. cumin
- a bit of water

INSTRUCTIONS

Mix together ingredients.  Shape into patties.  BBQ.

Alternatively, call Grandma and she’ll make them for you.
