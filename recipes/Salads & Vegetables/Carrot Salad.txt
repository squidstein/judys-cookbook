# Carrot Salad

Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                      Number of servings: 8 -10

INGREDIENTS

- Carrots – sliced
- Spinach
- Oil
- Paprika
- Cumin
- Vinegar
- garlic powder
- salt

INSTRUCTIONS

Wash spinach.  Cook with very little water.  Drain then cut up.  Set aside.

Heat oil.  Add paprika.  When heated through, add just enough water to cover sliced carrots.  Cook on medium heat.  When liquid is reduced add spinach, salt, garlic, cumin and vinegar.  Mix well.  Reduce liquid further if necessary.
