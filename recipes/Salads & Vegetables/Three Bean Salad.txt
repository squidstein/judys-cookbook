# Three Bean Salad

Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Sylvia’s Kitchen                           Number of servings: 8 -10

INGREDIENTS

- 1 – 19 oz can of black beans
- 1 – 19 oz can of chickpeas
- 1 – 19 oz can of lentils
- 2 – 4 cloves of garlic
- I green pepper chopped
- I red pepper chopped
- ¼ cup red onion, chopped
- ¼ cup coriander or cilantro
- ¼ cup extra virgin olive oil
- ¼ cup balsamic vinegar
- 1 tsp. ground cumin
- salt and pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Rinse all beans with cold water.

Mix together all ingredients and refrigerate overnight for more flavour.
