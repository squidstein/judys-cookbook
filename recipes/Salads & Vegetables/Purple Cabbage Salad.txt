# Purple Cabbage Salad

From Leslie’s Kitchen                                Preparation Time: ½ hr

INGREDIENTS

- Purple Cabbage
- Sliced pickled beets (Bicks)
- Red onion thinly sliced
- Salt
- Mayonnaise
- 1 tbsp. honey

INSTRUCTIONS

Thinly slice as much cabbage as you want.  Add as many sliced pickled beets, as you like.  Add the pickling vinegar from the jar proportionate to the amount of beets you’ve used.  For example, if you use half a jar of beets, use half the vinegar in the jar.  Throw in a LITTLE thinly sliced red onion, a little salt, a spoonful of honey and a couple of good dollops of mayo.  It is best to do it a couple of hours in advance so the flavours permeate the cabbage.
