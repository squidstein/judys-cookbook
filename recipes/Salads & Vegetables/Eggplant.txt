# Eggplant

Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                     Number of servings: 8 - 10

INGREDIENTS

- 2 eggplants
- salt
- garlic powder
- cumin
- oil
- 2 tbsp. vinegar

INSTRUCTIONS

Peel eggplant in alternating strips.  Cut off tops.

Cut into 1 inch cubes

Soak in salted water for 5 – 10 minutes

Boil in almost enough water to cover eggplant until soft.

Drain

In the same pot, heat oil.

Add paprika – heat through.

Add drained eggplant, salt, garlic powder, cumin and vinegar.

Mix well

Simmer for approximately 10 minutes.

Remove from heat.
