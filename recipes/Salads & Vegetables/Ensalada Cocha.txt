# Ensalada Cocha

Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen               Number of servings: appetizer

INGREDIENTS

- tomatoes – crushed, preferably fresh
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- oil
- paprika
- salt
- garlic powder
- cayenne pepper (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Heat oil, add paprika, and then add tomatoes and green peppers.

Cook on low heat until water has evaporated and sauce is reduced.

Add salt, garlic powder and cayenne and continue to simmer.
