# Beets

Preparation Time: 10 min
From Grandma’s Kitchen 		   Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- Fresh beets
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic powder
- Oil
- Lemon juice
- Parsley

INSTRUCTIONS

Place beets in pressure cooker. Cover with water.  Cook for 20 minutes.  Remove and peel under cool water.  Skins should slip off easily.  Slice.

Add condiments.

Mix gently.

Refrigerate.
