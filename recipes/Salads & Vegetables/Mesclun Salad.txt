# Mesclun Salad

with Lemon Vinaigrette and Asparagus spears

INGREDIENTS

- Mesclun salad greens
- Asparagus, pencil-size, 8 per person, cut approximately 4-inches long
- LEMON VINAIGRETTE:
- 1/4 cup extra-virgin olive oil
- 2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice
- 3/4 teaspoon minced fresh garlic
- 3/4 teaspoon Kosher salt
- freshly cracked black pepper
- 3/4 teaspoon fresh Thyme leaves

INSTRUCTIONS

Use just the tips of the asparagus, steam until tender crisp, remove from heat, drain and immediately plunge into ice water. Drain well again and toss gently with some of the lemon vinaigrette. Set aside.

For dressing: Gently stir the first five ingredients together, do not emulsify. Just before serving, add the fresh Thyme leaves.

To plate up, arrange 16 pencil-sized asparagus on each plate, Lincoln Log style. Place the mesclun in a large bowl and drizzle the vinaigrette over, tossing lightly. Place some of the tossed mesclun inside each "Lincoln Log" square, serve.
