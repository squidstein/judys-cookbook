# Bean Salad

Preparation Time: 10 min
Norene Gilletz                            		 Number of servings: 12

INGREDIENTS

- 19oz. (540 ml) can black beans
- 19oz. (540 ml) can chickpeas
- 19oz. (540 ml) can lentils
- 2 cloves garlic, crushed
- 1 green and/or red pepper, chopped
- 1 yellow red pepper, chopped
- ½ c. red onions, chopped
- ¼ c. coriander/cilantro, minced
- ¼ c. extra-virgin olive oil
- ¼ c. balsamic vinegar
- 1 tsp. ground cumin
- Salt & pepper to taste

INSTRUCTIONS

Rinse beans, chickpeas and lentils; drain well.  Combine all ingredients in a large mixing bowl; mix well. Season to taste.  Cover and refrigerate.  This taste even better the next day.

This salad will keep for up to 3 days in the refrigerator if tightly covered.
