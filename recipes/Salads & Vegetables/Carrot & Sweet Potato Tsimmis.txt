# Carrot & Sweet Potato Tsimmis

From Sylvia’s Kitchen – variation of Norene Gilletz pg. 328

INGREDIENTS

- 5 lbs. carrots
- 5 sweet potatoes
- salt
- 1 c. honey
- ¾ c. sugar
- ¼ c. oil
- 3 tbsp. potato starch
- 1 c. orange juice
- cinnamon (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Boil carrots for about 5 minutes.  Save water. For brighter colour, put drained carrots in ice water.  Boil sweet potatoes for 1 – 2 minutes.  Ice the same way.  Save water.

Layer carrots, sweet potatoes and prunes in aluminum pan.

Measure out 2 ladles of carrot water, 2 ladles of potato water into measuring cup and add honey, sugar, oil and ginger.  Dissolve potato starch into orange juice and add to hot mixture.  Add cinnamon, if using.

Pour over carrot mixture.

Cover with foil for 1st half hour of cooking.  Remove foil and flip over carefully to moisten top layers.  Continue cooking for another hour – uncovered.
