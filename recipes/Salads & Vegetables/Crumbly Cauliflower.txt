# Crumbly Cauliflower

Preparation Time: 10 min
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- cauliflower
- eggs
- bread crumbs seasoned with garlic powder, salt, and sugar.

INSTRUCTIONS

1. Cut cauliflower into large pieces. Steam cauliflower until tender, but not too soft. Drain and cool.

2. Dip each cauliflower piece in eggs, and then dip in seasoned bread crumbs.

3. Place in greased baking dish.

4. Bake at 350 degrees Fahrenheit for 20-30 minutes until heated through.
