# Peppers

Preparation Time: 10 min
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- Roasted Green and Red Peppers
- Season with:
- Salt
- Garlic powder
- Oil
- Lemon juice
- Roasted Hot Peppers
- Season with:
- Salt
- Garlic powder
- Oil
- NO lemon juice

INSTRUCTIONS

Mix gently.

Refrigerate.
