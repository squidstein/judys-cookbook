# Salad Dressing

From Judy Hazan’s Kitchen                   Preparation Time: 10 min

INGREDIENTS

- 2 parts oil (⅔ c.)
- 1 part apple cider vinegar (⅓ c.)
- 1 Tbsp. honey
- Salt
- Pepper
- 1 clove crushed Garlic
- 1 Tbsp. Tarragon
- ½ thinly sliced red onion

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine all ingredients, including red onion.  Let soak at least ½ hr. in advance.

Refrigerate.

Add any of the following to your salad:

Ripe avocado

Mandarin sections

Toasted almond slivers

Hearts of palm

Dried cranberries

Toasted pecans
