# Cornmeal Muffins

Preparation Time: ½hr
From Meshu’s Kitchen 			            Yield: 12 muffins

INGREDIENTS

- ¾ c. cornmeal
- 1¼ c. milk
- 1 c. flour
- 3 tsp. baking powder
- ⅓ c. white sugar
- ½ tsp. salt
- 1 egg, slightly beaten
- ¼ c. melted shortening

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 400°.

Grease 12 medium sized muffin cups.

Mix together cornmeal and milk and set aside for 5 minutes.

Sift together flour, baking powder, sugar and salt.

Stir slightly beaten egg and melted shortening into cornmeal mixture.

Add liquid to dry ingredients and stir only until combined (batter will be lumpy).

Fill muffin cups ⅔ full.

Bake at 400° for 20 – 25 minutes.
