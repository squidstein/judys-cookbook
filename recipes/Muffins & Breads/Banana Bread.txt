# Banana Bread

Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Meshu’s Kitchen                            		     Yield: 1 loaf

INGREDIENTS

- 1 c. sugar
- ½ c. butter
- 2 beaten eggs
- 3 mashed bananas
- 1¾ c. flour
- 1 tsp. baking soda
- 1 tsp. baking powder
- pinch of salt

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 325°.

Combine flour, baking powder, baking soda and salt, set aside.

Cream together butter and sugar.

Add eggs and bananas.

Add flour mixture.

Grease loaf pan and bake @ 325°, 1 hr 10 – 20 minutes.
