# Wheatgerm Muffins

From Meshu’s Kitchen 			            Yield: 12 muffins

INGREDIENTS

- 1⅔ c. Cake & Pastry Flour
- ¾ c. Wheat Germ
- 3Tbsp. white sugar
- 5 tsp. baking powder
- ½ tsp. salt
- 2 eggs, slightly beaten
- ⅓ c. oil
- 1 c. milk

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 400°.

Grease 12 medium sized muffin cups.

Mix together dry ingredients.

Sift together flour, wheat germ, baking powder, sugar and salt.

Add liquid to dry ingredients and stir only until combined (batter will be lumpy).

Fill muffin cups ⅔ full.

Bake at 400° for 15 - 20 minutes.
