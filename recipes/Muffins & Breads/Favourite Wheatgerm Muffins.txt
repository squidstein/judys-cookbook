# Favourite Wheatgerm Muffins

From Meshu’s Kitchen 			            Yield: 12 muffins

INGREDIENTS

- 1 c. all purpose Flour
- 1 c. toasted Wheat Germ
- 4 tsp. baking powder
- ¼ tsp. baking soda
- ½ tsp. salt
- 1 egg
- 2 T. soften butter or margarine
- ¼ c. packed brown sugar
- grated zest of one orange
- ¼ c orange juice
- ¾ c. plain yogurt

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 425°.

Grease 12 medium sized muffin cups.

In a large bowl combine flour, wheat germ, baking powder, baking soda and salt.  Make a well in the centre

In another bowl cream together egg, butter, and sugar.  Stir in zest and orange juice, mixing well.  Add yogurt. Add to dry ingredients, stirring until just blended.  Do not over mix

Pour batter into prepared muffin tins

Bake for 15 minutes.
