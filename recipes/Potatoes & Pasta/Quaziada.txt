# Quaziada

(English Subtitle – Potato and Egg Pie)
Preparation Time: 1 hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- 5 lbs. potatoes
- 7 eggs
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- ½ tsp. turmeric
- parsley
- water from boiled potatoes

INSTRUCTIONS

Boil potatoes in water without salt.

Beat potatoes.  Add eggs, salt and spices.

(Do not over whip, they will become like glue)

Heat oil in pan.  When heated add potato mixture and bake at 350° until golden brown (about 1 hour).

Grandma usually makes this for her Yom Kippur dinner.
