# Pasta Salad

Preparation Time: 1 hr
Leslie’s Kitchen                                     Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- penne or rotini				zucchini
- 2 –3 Tbsp olive oil			mushrooms
- lots of crushed garlic			red or green peppers
- 1 jar of Classico pasta sauce 		red onion
- (Roasted Red Pepper or Sundried Tomato)	artichoke hearts
- canned sliced black olives		sundried tomatoes
- fresh basil (if you have it)		salt & pepper
- toasted pine nuts (optional)		crushed chilies
- fresh Parmesan cheese (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Boil noodles in well-salted water – drain (save a little water) and cool a little. Sauté lots of crushed garlic in olive oil and whatever vegetables you want to add that require cooking, i.e. zucchini, mushrooms, red or green pepper, red onion.  Don’t cook until mushy – just a couple of minutes.  Dump in a jar of pasta sauce.  Add black olives, artichoke hearts and sundried tomatoes.  Add fresh basil if you have it.  Also add toasted pine nuts and fresh Parmesan, if using.

Make sure it’s not too dry.  If so, add more sauce if you need or a little of the pasta water and maybe a little more olive oil.

Add salt, pepper and crushed chilies, to taste.
