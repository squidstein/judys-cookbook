# Kugel

Preparation Time: ½ hr
Francine’s Kitchen                                 Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- ½ lb. cream cheese
- ½ lb. pressed cottage cheese
- ¼ lb. (½ c) butter
- ½ c. sugar
- 4 eggs
- 1 bag medium egg noodles
- ½ c. cornflake crumbs
- 1 Tbsp. melted butter

INSTRUCTIONS

Boil noodles.  Drain.  Mix first 5 ingredients.  Toss with noodles. Mix together melted butter and cornflake crumbs.  Top noodle mixture with cornflake crumbs topping. Bake in oven 45 minutes @ 350°.

Cornflakes are not peanut free.  If necessary, replace with Rice Krispie crumbs.
