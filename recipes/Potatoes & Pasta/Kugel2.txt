# Kugel2

Preparation Time: 1 hr
Sylvia’s mother-in-law 		           Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- 12 oz. pkg. cooked medium egg noodles
- 6 eggs
- ½ c melted butter or margarine
- (½ for mixture, ½ for greasing)
- 1½ lb. pressed cottage cheese
- 1 tsp. salt
- 1 c. sugar
- 1 c. cold milk

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 350°. Separate eggs.  Melt butter or margarine.

Using electric mixer, mix cottage cheese together with egg yolks and salt until well blended.  Add cooked noodles and blend thoroughly.  Add salt sugar and half of the melted margarine and blend together.

Bat egg whites until stiff but not dry.  Fold them into the cottage cheese mixture.

Place mixture in 9”x13” greased baking dish (remaining butter).  Pour the cold mils over the mixture and bake for 1 hour @ 350°, until golden.
