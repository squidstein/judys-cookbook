# Mediterranean Potatoes

EMBED Word.Picture.8
Preparation Time: ½hr
From: Meshu’s Kitchen	                          Number of servings: 6-8

INGREDIENTS

- 2 lbs potatoes, peeled & halved
- ¼ c. oil
- 3-4 crushed garlic cloves
- salt & pepper
- 2 tsp. sweet paprika
- 1 tbsp. oregano
- ½ tsp. cayenne
- 2 tbsp. red wine vinegar
- ½ tin tomato paste
- 2 c’s water

INSTRUCTIONS

Sauté garlic in oil.  After it starts sizzling, add paprika then add water.  Do not let the paprika overheat before adding water.   Add salt and pepper and oregano.  Add potatoes, cover and let cook until tender.  Combine red wine vinegar with tomato paste and cayenne pepper add to potatoes.  Continue to cook for 5 – 10 minutes.  Sauce should be thickened enough to coat potatoes.

I serve this with Chicken Souvlaki.
