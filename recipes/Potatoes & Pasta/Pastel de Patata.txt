# Pastel de Patata

(English Subtitle – Sheffers Pie)
Preparation Time: 1½ hrs
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- 5 lbs. potatoes
- 5 eggs, hardboiled
- 1¼ lb. lean ground beef
- 1 large onion
- ½ tsp. turmeric
- salt, to taste
- pepper
- nutmeg
- parsley
- oil
- 1 lemon, freshly squeezed
- water from boiled potatoes

INSTRUCTIONS

Cook ground beef with ¼ c of water and finely chopped onion, pepper, nutmeg, parsley and onion.  (Kosher meat is already salted)

Boil potatoes in water without salt

Slice hard boiled eggs

Mash potatoes (do not overwhip, they will become like glue); add turmeric, a bit of oil, lemon juice and gradually add water potatoes were boiled in, until you reach the desired consistency for the potatoes.

Layer starting with potatoes, then ground beef, and then sliced eggs, ending with a later of potatoes.  Spray some oil on top for browning.  Decorate with fork and bake in oven at 375° until browned (about 1 hour 20 min).
