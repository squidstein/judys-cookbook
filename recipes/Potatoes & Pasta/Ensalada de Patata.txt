# Ensalada de Patata

(English Subtitle – Potato Salad)
Preparation Time: 1hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                     Number of servings: 10-12

INGREDIENTS

- 5 lbs. red potatoes
- 8 eggs, hardboiled
- 2 tins of tuna, drained
- salt
- pepper
- 1 large onion
- oil
- 1 lemon, freshly squeezed
- vinegar

INSTRUCTIONS

Cut potatoes into bite sized cubes.  Boil gently in well salted water.  Be careful not to overcook.  Pierce with fork, when tender, strain and allow to cool.

Put onion, parsley, oil, lemon juice vinegar and pepper into food processor and blend gently (do not add more salt).

Mix cooled potatoes with vinaigrette.

Place potato mixture into bowl or platter and top with chopped egg.  Garnish with crushed tuna.
