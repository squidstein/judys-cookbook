# Chicken Wings

Prep. Time: 15 mins
From Grandma’s Kitchen 		Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- Chicken wings
- Salt
- Pepper
- Garlic powder
- Honey
- Hoisen Sauce (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Preheat oven to 300°.  Place chicken wings in a single layer in a roasting pan.  Do not add any water.

Place in preheated oven.  After ½ hour, if chicken is cooked through, add honey and hoisen sauce and return to oven for 15 – 30 minutes, until golden.

On the off chance that there are leftovers, they freeze very well.
