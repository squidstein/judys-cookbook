# Chicken Curry

From Leslie’s Kitchen 			Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- 5-6 boneless chicken breasts – diced big
- 3 cloves garlic - crushed
- 2 onions
- 1/3 jar Patak’s curry paste-med, hot or madras
- 1 can diced tomatoes
- 2 c. chicken broth
- 2-3 c. diced white potato
- 1 cauliflower fair size chunks
- 2 c. chopped cabbage
- 1 jar PC madras and butter chicken sauce
- 3 T. chunky peanut butter
- 4 T. Patak’s mango chutney
- Either 1 can of coconut milk or a handful of sweetened shredded coconut or both.

INSTRUCTIONS

Sauté diced chicken with garlic and onions. Add 1/3 jar Patak’s curry paste-med., hot or madras. Continue sautéing until chicken is about ½ cooked.

Add can diced tomatoes-cook another couple minutes.

Add 2 c. chicken broth, and veggies.

Add 1 jar PC madras and butter chicken sauce

and peanut butter.

Add Patak’s mango chutney at the end and either 1 can of coconut milk or a handful of sweetened shredded coconut or both.

Whole thing simmers for about 1 hr.-start to finish

Serve with plain basmati rice OR use basmati rice, add 1 chopped onion,

2 T. butter and 1 T. Patak’s biryani paste and cook as usual.

Round out the meal with samosas, pakoras, papadum, mango chutney, raita.

Most ingredients are found at Loblaws/Fortinos.

If you decide to get adventurous there is an Indian food store in the plaza at Dufferin and Steeles (N/W)
