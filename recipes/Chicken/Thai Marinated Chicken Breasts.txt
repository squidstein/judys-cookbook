# Thai Marinated Chicken Breasts

From Bonnie Stern’s Kitchen                      Number of servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 3 tbsp lime juice
- 2 tbsp vegetable oil
- 2 tsp minced fresh ginger
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- Pinch hot red chili flakes
- 2 tbsp chopped fresh coriander
- Salt

INSTRUCTIONS

Trim any fat from chicken.  Pat dry. Leave as is or flatten by removing the small file, then placing between sheets of waxed paper and pounding to ½-inch thickness.

In small bowl, combine lime juice, oil, ginger, garlic, chili flakes and coriander.

In shallow dish, combine chicken breast with marinade, cover and marinate 1 to 8 hours in refrigerator.

Sprinkle with salt.  Cook on barbecue, in grill pan, under broiler or in non-stick pan.  Cook regular chicken breasts 5 to 6 minutes per side or flattened ones 3 to 4 minutes per side or until cooked through.

Sprinkle with sesame seeds.
