# Moroccan Marinated Chicken Breasts

From Bonnie Stern’s Kitchen                      Number of servings: 4

INGREDIENTS

- 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
- 3 tbsp lemon juice
- 2 tbsp olive oil
- 1 tbsp honey
- 1 tsp each: ground cumin, paprika
- ¼ tsp. cinnamon
- Pinch cayenne
- 2 garlic cloves
- Salt
- 1 tbsp sesame seeds, lightly toasted

INSTRUCTIONS

Trim any fat from chicken.  Pat dry. Leave as is or flatten by removing the small file, then placing between sheets of waxed paper and pounding to ½-inch thickness.

In small bowl, combine lemon juice, oil, honey, cumin, paprika, cinnamon, cayenne and garlic.

In shallow dish, combine chicken breast with marinade, cover and marinate 1 to 8 hours in refrigerator.

Sprinkle with salt.  Cook in non-stick pan, in grill pan, on barbecue or under broiler.  Cook regular chicken breasts 5 to 6 minutes per side or flattened ones 3 to 4 minutes per side or until cooked through.

Sprinkle on sesame seeds.

Serve this dish with couscous combined with olives and lemon or raisins and nuts.
