# Fish Loaf - Sylvia's

Preparation Time: 1hr
From Sylvia’s Kitchen                                 Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- 2 lbs. ground fish
- 2 large carrots
- 1 medium spanish onion
- ¼ c. oil
- 3 eggs
- ¾ c. water
- 2½ tsp. salt
- pepper to taste
- 1 tsp. sugar
- 3 tbsp. matzo meal

INSTRUCTIONS

Grate carrots into long strips and finely chop onion.  Sauté lightly until translucent – approx. 20 minutes on low, then cool.

Add remaining ingredients into mixer and mix for 3 – 4 minutes.  Add carrots and onion.

Spray glass loaf pan or if using a metal pan, line first with wax paper then spray.

Bake at 350° for 1hr 15 min (add 10 minutes if necessary)

Cool 15 minutes in loaf pan then remove over the sink, up side down in hand to drain any remaining liquid.

Cool up side down for ½ hour then turn right side up again.  Cover with Saran wrap and refrigerate.
