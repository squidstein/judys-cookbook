# Pescado al horno

(English Subtitle – Fish in the oven)
Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                       Number of servings: 6 - 8

INGREDIENTS

- potatoes
- sole filets
- tomatoes, sliced
- onion, sliced
- lemon, sliced
- 1 tsp. table salt
- pepper to taste
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ½ tsp. turmeric
- 3 tbsp. oil

INSTRUCTIONS

Grease glass Pyrex dish.  Slice potatoes, onions and lemon.  Layer, starting with potato, sole fillets, lemon slices, tomatoes then finally onion slices.

In a pan, heat oil with turmeric; add salt pepper, garlic and a bit of water.  Hat through then pour over potatoes and fish layers.

Cover with foil and bake 45 minutes at 350°.

Remove foil and brown slightly.
