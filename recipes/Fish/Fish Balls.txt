# Fish Balls

Preparation Time: ½ hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen 	           Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- 2 lbs. ground fish
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp. table salt
- pepper to taste
- ½ tsp garlic powder
- ¾ tsp. cumin
- 1 tbsp. dried parsley
- 3 tbsp. matzo meal
- 1 tin Hunts tomato sauce
- ½ tin water
- 1 tbsp. paprika
- 3 tbsp. oil

INSTRUCTIONS

Mix together first 8 ingredients.

Form balls.

Heat oil with paprika in pot.  Do not overheat.  Add tin of Hunts tomato sauce plus ½ tin of water.  Bring to a mild boil.  Add fishballs.  When boil breaks again, reduce heat to medium and cover.

Cook 20 – 30 minutes.
