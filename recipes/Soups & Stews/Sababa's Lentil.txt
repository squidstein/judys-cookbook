# Sababa's Lentil

Preparation Time: ½hr
Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- 1¼ cups red lentils
- ½ cup white rice
- ⅓ cup vegetable oil
- 1 large yellow onion, chopped
- 6 cups warm water
- 1 tbsp. vegetable stock
- 1 tbsp. ground cumin
- 1 tbsp. olive oil
- 1 tbsp. salt or to taste
- Lemon wedges, optional

INSTRUCTIONS

Soak lentils and rice in warm water.  Add onion to hot vegetable oil in large saucepan.  Add the 6 cups of water and bring to a boil.

Drain soaking lentils and rice. Rinse well then add to boiling water with onion and boil 15 to 20 minutes.

Add vegetable stock, cumin olive oil and salt.  Serve each bowl with wedge of lemon.

Makes 8 cups.
