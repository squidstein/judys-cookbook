# Beet Borscht

Preparation Time: 1hr
From Camila’s Kitchen 			   Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- 1 pc. beef shank
- 10 c. julienned beets		2 tsp. kosher salt
- 1 large diced potato		⅓ c. lemon juice
- 1 diced onion			3 minced garlic cloves
- 4 julienned carrots		48 oz can tomato juice
- fresh dill			sugar (optional)

INSTRUCTIONS

Cook meat in 10-12 cups cold water in pressure cooker. After it cooks, I like to pour it through a strainer into another pot, with the meat. Cut up green beans, potatoes, onion, carrots and beets.  Add to pot with salt.  Gently boil until beets are tender.

Add tomato juice, lemon juice, garlic and fresh dill.  Continue to boil gently for 10 minutes.

Serve with chopped fresh dill.

This can be made as “dairy” by not adding meat.  When serving, add chopped fresh dill and sour cream.
