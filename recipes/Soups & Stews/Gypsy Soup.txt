# Gypsy Soup

From Leslie’s Kitchen 			Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- 2 T olive oil
- 2 cups chopped onions
- 3 cloves garlic
- 2 stalks celery, chopped
- 2 cups diced sweet potato
- 1 tsp salt
- 1 tsp cinnamon
- 1/2 tsp cayenne
- 2 tsp turmeric
- 2 T basil
- 2 T paprika
- 3 bay leaves
- 1 large tin tomato juice
- 1 can diced tomatoes or 2 fresh tomatoes
- 1 can chick peas
- 2-3 cups frozen corn
- 1 green pepper chopped
- 1 chopped zucchini
- Any other veggies you feel like adding: cauliflower, green beans, peas, even broccoli but add
- at the very end of cooking to keep crisp

INSTRUCTIONS

Sauté onions, celery, garlic and sweet potatoes in oil, about 10 min.

Add spices and sauté another minute or so.

Add tomatoes and juice, simmer until sweet potatoes are almost tender.

Add other veggies and simmer until potatoes are done and other vegetable are still tender-crisp.

Can serve with shredded tex-mex cheese and sour cream.
