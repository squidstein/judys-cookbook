# Lentejas

Preparation Time: 15 mins
From Grandma’s Kitchen                       Number of servings: 8-10

INGREDIENTS

- 2 c. lentils
- 1 onions, chopped
- 1 tomato, chopped
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- salt
- pepper
- garlic
- oil
- 2 – 3 bay leaves
- water (½ pot)

INSTRUCTIONS

Clean and rinse lentils

Bring water to a boil and then add lentils, tomato, onion green pepper, bay leaves, garlic and pepper.  Return to boil. Lower to medium heat.  Cover with lid.

When lentils open and are tender, add salt and oil.

Remove bay leaves.

Partially blend with hand blender.
