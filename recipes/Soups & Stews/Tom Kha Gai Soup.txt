# Tom Kha Gai Soup

From Leslie’s Kitchen                            Preparation Time: 1hr

INGREDIENTS

- 1 pkg. “A Taste of Thai Coconut Ginger Soup Mix”
- 2 Boneless chicken breasts
- Sliced mushrooms
- 1 –2 cans of coconut milk (start with 1 add another if desired)
- Juice from 1 lime (or to taste)
- 2 tbsp. PC Fiery Thai Sauce
- Don’t use fish sauce, as the package directs and add half chicken stock, half water (package calls for either).

INSTRUCTIONS

Start with a package of “A Taste of Thai Coconut Ginger Soup Mix”.  Follow recipe on back with a couple of minor changes.  Thinly slice and sauté a couple of boneless chicken breasts.  Add some sliced mushrooms to the same pan at the end and sauté as well.  Stir in the contents of one or both envelopes.  One for 4 people, etc.  Add two cans of coconut milk, juice from 1 lime (or to taste), and a couple of tablespoons of PC Fiery Thai Sauce.  DO NOT use fish sauce as the package directs and add half chicken, half water (package calls for either).
