# Pea Soup

Preparation Time: 1hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                            Number of servings: 6

INGREDIENTS

- 2 c. yellow peas			salt
- 2 c. green peas			pepper
- 1 onion, quartered			garlic powder
- 4 carrots				oil
- 2 potatoes, cubed
- 3 celery stalks, chopped

INSTRUCTIONS

Clean peas. Rinse.

Peas foam when cooked, so choose a pot that allow room for expansion.

Boil water, add vegetables and peas.  Mix well.  When it comes back to a boil, reduce heat to “3”.  Cover (leaving edge of pot slightly exposed).  Continue cooking for about 1 hour.  Mix with Moulinex.

Add salt, garlic powder, salt, pepper and a bit of oil.
