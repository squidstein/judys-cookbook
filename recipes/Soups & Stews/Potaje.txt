# Potaje

(English Subtitle – Navy Bean Soup)
Preparation Time: 1hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                         Number of servings: 6-8

INGREDIENTS

- 4 cups white navy beans
- 2 onions
- oil
- paprika
- water
- squash
- garlic powder
- cayenne pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

Clean white navy beans.

As soon as boil breaks, reduce heat to “3” and cover pot.

For ½ hour do not lift lid.  It will soften then check water.  If needed, add boiling water.  Add garlic powder and a bit of cayenne pepper.
