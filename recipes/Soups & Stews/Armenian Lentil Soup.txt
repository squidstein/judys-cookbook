# Armenian Lentil Soup

From Moosewood Cookbook

INGREDIENTS

- 1½ c. dried lentils (Judy uses red)
- 6 c water or veg stock (parve chicken broth)
- ½ c. chopped apricots
- 3 – 4 Tbsp. vegetable oil
- 1 c. chopped onions
- 2 – 3 c. cubed peeled eggplant
- 1½ c. chopped tomato
- 1 green pepper, chopped
- ¼ tsp. cinnamon
- ¼ tsp. allspice
- ¼ tsp. cayenne or crushed red pepper
- 1 Tbsp. paprika
- 1½ tsp. salt
- 3 Tbsp chopped fresh parsley
- 1 Tbsp chopped fresh mint

INSTRUCTIONS

Rinse lentils, add water and bring to boil.  Cover and reduce heat and simmer for 20 minutes.  Add chopped apricots.  Simmer another 20 minutes. (40 mins in total)

Sauté onion in oil then add chopped eggplant with 5 tbsps of water.  Cook covered at medium heat, stirring occasionally (approx 20 minutes).

Add other vegetables, salt and spices to eggplant mixture and continue to cook for another 10 minutes.

Stir this mixture into the lentils.

Continue to cook for another 15 minutes. Serve with parsley and mint as garnish.
