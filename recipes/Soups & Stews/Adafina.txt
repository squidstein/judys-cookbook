# Adafina

(English Subtitle – Moroccan sleep-eze)
Preparation Time: 1hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                         Number of servings: 6-8

INGREDIENTS

- flanken or brisket
- 6 eggs
- 15 potatoes
- 1 c. chickpeas
- 1½ tsp salt
- ¼ tsp cinnamon
- ¼ tsp turmeric
- ½ tsp nutmeg
- 2 tbsp honey

INSTRUCTIONS

Soak chickpeas overnight

Boil eggs separately

Boil meat for ½ hour in pot filled 1/3 of water.  Add salt and spices.  Add drained chickpeas, eggs, and potatoes.  There should only be enough water to cover potatoes.

Put in the oven at covered at 225°

(Friday night at 5 – 6 pm)

Check in the morning.  If there is too much water, leave uncovered until lunch.

A real crowd pleaser
