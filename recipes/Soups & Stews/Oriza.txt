# Oriza

Preparation Time: 1hr
From Grandma’s Kitchen                         Number of servings: 6-8

INGREDIENTS

- 1 lb. cubed meat
- 1lb. soft wheat kernels
- 2 small onions
- 1 head of garlic
- 2 sweet potatoes
- salt
- 1 Tbsp. paprika
- oil

INSTRUCTIONS

Wash and soak wheat overnight.

Peel and parboil sweet potatoes.

Fry 2 small onions in a little oil.

Remove onions and fry cubed meat in the same pot.

Put onions back and add salt and paprika.

Separate 1 head of garlic – DO NOT peel.

Mix everything together except sweet potatoes.

Arrange potatoes on top.

Cover with water.

Bake at 275° - 300° for about 17 hours.

(Friday night at 5 – 6 pm)
