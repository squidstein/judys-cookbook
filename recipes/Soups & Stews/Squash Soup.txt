# Squash Soup

Preparation Time: ½hr
From Judy’s Kitchen                                   Number of servings: 8

INGREDIENTS

- Potatoes			salt
- Carrots			pepper
- Celery				1 tbsp. dried basil
- Onion				½ tsp. cinnamon
- squash (more)			¼ tsp. turmeric
- pumpkin (less)			2 tbsp. honey
- 2 tbsp. chicken broth		2 tbsp. margarine

INSTRUCTIONS

Combine all the ingredients in a pressure cooker with enough water to cover vegetables.  Cook for 15 minutes after pot starts to whistle.

Process in blender until smooth.

For added richness, ½ cup table cream may be added after ingredients are well blended.
